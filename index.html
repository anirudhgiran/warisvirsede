ABOUT

A Brampton based dance academy bringing you traditional form of Punjabi folk dance "BHANGRA" and "GIDDHA". We aim to bring people from all walks of life more closer to our roots, culture and tranditions while keeping it easy and fun. We organize annual folk festival event and pariticipte in many events across Canada and even internationally to bring our culture and talent to the world. Here at Waris Virse De, we pride ourselves on being able to provide an oppurtunity where we can all come together to embrace our love and passion for Bhangra. We also teach other various folk dance forms like Luddi, Jhoomer, Malwai Giddha, Jindua, and many more that you many not have yet heard of! Our teaching team is very seasoned and has participated at international levels and won serveral medals, and even judged Bhangra competetions.
Check out the website for more details on the classes we offer.

So why wait come and join us!
